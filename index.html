So... what can I do in the JE Pressroom?

If you're a visual learner, here's a fun video showing all the artistry that goes on in the pressroom. In short, we specialize in letterpress printing. It's a craft that slows down life, something that we all need more of. Create beautiful prints, holiday cards, and the most personalized gifts. Learn a craft that requires patience, meet other creatives in a welcoming environment & get hands-on with learning! In this spring, our wonderful Jesse Marsolais teaches a letterpress printing class for credit called Art of The Printed Word, open to all undergraduate students.
